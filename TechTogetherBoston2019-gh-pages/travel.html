Car directions_car

Gas will be reimbursed for any car that has 3 or more hackers in it. To qualify as a hacker, each person must:

  • Be registered
  • Checked in during the event
  • Have submitted a project to DevPost during the judging period

Please check the chart to see how much of your gas we may be able to cover. We have limited funds, so reimbursement may be provided on a first-come-first-serve basis.

Traveling Distance to Boston (roundtrip) Maximum Reimbursement (roundtrip)
0~50 miles $0
50~100 miles Up to $20
100~200 miles Up to $40
200~300 miles Up to $60
300~400 miles Up to $80
400+ miles Up to $100

Other Inquiries directions_subway flight

We may be able to reimburse you for other modes of transportation. Please check the chart to see what you may be qualified for. All these requests will be considered on a case-by-case basis. In order to be reimbursed, you must:

  • Retain your proof of purchase for flight/train/bus tickets
  • Submit your project to our DevPost
  • Fill out our post hackathon survey after TechTogether Boston

We will not be able to reimburse you if you don't fulfill all these requirements.

Traveling Distance to Boston (one-way) Maximum Reimbursement (roundtrip)
0~800 miles Up to $40
900~1600 miles Up to $80
1700~2400 miles Up to $100
2500~3100 miles Up to $120
3200+ miles Up to $200
If you have any questions, email us at info@techtogether.io.
