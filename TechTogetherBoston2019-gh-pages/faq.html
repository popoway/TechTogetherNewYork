FAQ

What is a hackathon? What do I do at a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event that gathers individuals with different abilities to create a project in a limited amount of time. At TechTogether, you can build anything you want (an app, website, robot, etc.) and you will have the help of workshops and mentors!

How do I register?

You can register for TechTogether with the form here! We are currently accepting applicants on a rolling basis, so the sooner you apply, the better!

Where and when is TechTogether?

TechTogether will be held in Agganis Arena on March 22-24, 2019.

How much will it cost to attend?

This event, including all food, swag, and prizes, is completely free!

Who can attend?

We welcome female and non-binary individuals of all skill levels who have a passion for learning more about technology and are 16 years or older.

What if I've never coded before?

We welcome beginners and experts alike! There will be plenty of workshops and mentors available to guide you throughout your 36-hour hacking journey, and you can also learn from other students!

Can I sleep? Where will I sleep?

Yes, of course! We will provide a space for you to sleep.

What should I bring?

Please bring an ID for admission and a laptop to hack on! We will send out a detailed list of what to pack closer to the hackathon.

How do teams work?

If you do not already have a team or project for the hackathon, do not worry! We will match you with other students with interests and skill levels similar to yours based off your answers to our application. The maximum team size is 5 members.

Yes! Information for travel reimbursement can be found here through this link.
