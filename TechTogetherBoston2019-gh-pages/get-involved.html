Get Involved

Contribute to TechTogether and help support our mission!

Volunteer

As a volunteer, you'll help with taking care of operations during the hackathon and create a smooth-sailing experience for all our hackers! We welcome anyone regardless of gender to volunteer.


Volunteer Benefits:
  • Special Volunteer SWAG
  • Priority choosing for all free meals
  • Behind the scenes experience of hackathon operations

Mentor

As a mentor, you'll help our hackers of all experience levels build their dream project! We welcome experts from academia or from any sector in tech to mentor at TechTogether Boston. We welcome anyone regardless of gender to mentor.


Mentor Benefits:
  • T-Shirt
  • Special Mentor SWAG
  • Feature on website

Ambassador

As a brand ambassador, you'll support our mission by spreading the word about our inclusive, welcoming hackathon! We welcome anyone regardless of gender to be an ambassador.


Ambassador Merit-Based Rewards:
  • Google Home Mini
  • Special Ambassador SWAG + STEMINIST Sweatshirt
  • Feature on website

Judge

As a judge on our panel, you'll have the opportunity to look at all of the amazing projects that our hackers have created and interact with participants when they are giving their awesome demos!

We welcome all people that have a strong technical background to join our judging panel.

Judge Benefits:
  • Special Judge SWAG
  • Priority choosing for all free meals
  • Behind the scenes experience of hackathon operations

PreHacks people

Our unique opportunity for High Schoolers to connect with mentors before the hackathon!

We welcome High Schoolers to join us for PreHacks, a workshop like event taking place in the afternoon on March 22nd, 2019 to meet mentors and prepare for TechTogether Boston. At PreHacks, students will get the opportunity to:

  • Learn more about what to expect and how to prepare for a hackathon
  • Get insight about the college experience at Boston University and its technical programs
  • Connect 1-on-1 with mentors that will help answer any questions related to careers in the tech industry

Applications for PreHacks are coming soon! If you have any questions about PreHacks, you can email prehacks@bu.edu for more details.

At TechTogether, our community is our most important attribute. We aspire to create an inclusive, empowering and supportive environment for our internal and external communities. We are always looking for diverse individuals who want to support our mission and be part of our cause!
For any further questions regarding involvement, email us at info@techtogether.io.
